Crazy Shooters

By FreezeNova
August 24, 2020

Crazy Shooters is a first-person shooter game where you have at your disposal many weapons to use during a match including an RPG, 4 highly-detailed maps to play and a lot of fun.Create your own room or chose one that is already created by someone else and be prepared to shoot down any hostiles that stand in your way.

Features:
• 4 Maps
• Multiplayer game rooms
• Great graphics

Controls:
• WASD or arrow keys to move
• Left mouse button to fire
• Right mouse button to aim
• R to reload
• Space to jump
• Hold shift to run
• 1-9 key to change weapon
• T to open chat and Enter to send
• C to crouch
